Street 77 Partners is an owner-operated investment firm focused on acquiring and operating a single healthcare or wellness business and growing it thoughtfully over the long term.

Unlike traditional private equity firms, Street 77 Partners invests with a patient, relationship-driven approach and a deep respect for founder legacy.

Who I Am

I am a healthcare management professional with nearly two decades of experience managing and scaling healthcare businesses, including organizations with up to $1 billion in revenue.

I come from a family of entrepreneurs, where the importance of hard work, integrity, and taking care of people was instilled early on. I earned my MBA from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business, where my entrepreneurial journey began to take shape.

In 2023, after welcoming my daughter, the desire to build something lasting — and to steward a business with care for the long term — became clear. That motivation led to the creation of Street 77 Partners.

My goal is to acquire a business I can operate for the rest of my professional life, preserving what makes it special while supporting its next chapter of growth.

Approach

Street 77 Partners is designed for owners who want more than just a transaction.

  • Direct communication with owners
  • Thoughtful, transparent processes
  • Fair and reliable outcomes
  • Long-term ownership, not financial engineering

We invest our own capital and operate with accountability and care.

What I Look For

Street 77 Partners is seeking a well-established small business in the healthcare or wellness space where the owner is considering retirement or transitioning away from day-to-day operations.

  • Strong reputation and loyal customer base
  • Committed, long-tenured teams
  • $400K+ in consistent cash flow
  • Opportunities for steady, sustainable growth

What I Provide

A Reliable and Respectful Transition

  • A clear and dependable exit aligned with personal or retirement goals
  • Capital to ensure business continuity and stability
  • A hands-on, experienced operator committed to long-term success

Above all, we are committed to treating employees, customers, and the broader community with respect and care.
